Kerala Higher Education

Description

  • This is a website that provides information about various scholarships offered by the Higher Education Department of Kerala, India.
  • The website lists the eligibility criteria, application procedure, selection process, and benefits of different scholarships for students pursuing higher education in Kerala.
  • The website also has links to other related websites such as the HEC Scholarship portal1, the Kerala State Higher Education Council, and the Admissions portal.
  • The website aims to help students access quality education and financial assistance in Kerala.

How to use them

  • Visit the website
  • On the homepage, you will see a list of scholarships under different categories such as merit, post-matric, minority, etc. You can click on any scholarship to view its details.
  • You can also use the search box on the top right corner to find a specific scholarship by entering its name or keyword.
  • Once you find the scholarship you are interested in, you can read the eligibility criteria, application procedure, selection process, and benefits of the scholarship. You can also download the application form or apply online if available.
  • You can also check the status of your application, view the list of selected candidates, and contact the concerned authorities for any queries or grievances. You can find the relevant links and contact details on the website.
  • You can also visit the other related websites such as the HEC Scholarship portal, the Kerala State Higher Education Council, and the Admissions portal for more information and opportunities.
