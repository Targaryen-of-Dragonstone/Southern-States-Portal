Kerala State Bank

Description

    This site is the official website of the Kerala State Co-operative Bank Ltd, also known as Kerala Bank. The site provides information and services related to the banking and financial activities of the bank, such as: The various deposit schemes, loan products, and micro finance programs offered by the bank for personal, agricultural, MSME, and pravasi sectors¹ The online applications and portals for various examinations and scholarships, such as K-TET, KITE, DHSE, SCOLE, etc. The government orders and circulars issued by the bank on various matters related to the banking and co-operative sector The site aims to facilitate the effective and transparent functioning of the bank and to ensure the quality and equity of banking services for all customers in Kerala.

How to Use:

  • Visit the site and explore the various sections and links on the homepage.
  • You can find information about the history, vision, mission, and objectives of the Kerala State Co-operative Bank Ltd, also known as Kerala Bank, as well as the various deposit schemes, loan products, and micro finance programs offered by the bank for personal, agricultural, MSME, and pravasi sectors.
  • You can also access the government orders and circulars issued by the bank on various matters related to the banking and co-operative sector by clicking on the “GOs & Circulars” link.
  • You can also apply and manage various banking services online, such as internet banking, mobile banking, ATM card, NEFT/RTGS, etc. by clicking on the “Online Services” link.
  • You can also contact the bank and give your feedback by clicking on the “Contact Us” link.

Treasury Savings Bank

Description

    This site is the official website of the Treasury Savings Bank (TSB) of Kerala. The site provides information and services related to the TSB accounts, such as:
  • The types of savings bank accounts offered by the TSB, such as TSB, PTSB, TFD, ETSB, etc.
  • The online registration and login process for the TSB account holders.
  • The online banking services, such as balance enquiry, fund transfer, statement download, etc.
  • The contact details and feedback form of the TSB.
  • The site aims to facilitate the effective and transparent functioning of the TSB and to ensure the quality and equity of banking services for all customers in Kerala.

    • How to use them

    • Visit the site and click on the “Register” button on the top right corner of the homepage.
    • Fill in the required details and create your account.
    • Log in to your account using your email and password.
    • Choose the type of savings bank account that you want to open and click on “Apply Online”.
    • Fill in the application form with your personal and bank details and submit it.
    • You can also access the online banking services, such as balance enquiry, fund transfer, statement download, etc. by clicking on the “Online Services” link.
    • You can also contact the TSB and give your feedback by clicking on the “Contact Us” link.
