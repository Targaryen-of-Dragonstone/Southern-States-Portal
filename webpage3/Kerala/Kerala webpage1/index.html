Department of General Education

Description

    This site is the official website of the General Education Department of the Government of Kerala. The site provides information and services related to the school education in the state, such as:
  • The vision, mission, and objectives of the department1
  • The various schemes and initiatives undertaken by the department, such as Samagra, Samanwaya, Mid Day Meal, School Kalolsavam, School Sports, Scholarships, School Sasthrolsavam, etc.
  • The government orders and circulars issued by the department on various matters related to the school education
  • The online applications and portals for various examinations and scholarships, such as K-TET, KITE, DHSE, SCOLE, etc.
  • The contact details and feedback form of the department
    • The site aims to facilitate the effective and transparent functioning of the department and to ensure the quality and equity of education for all children in Kerala

How to Use:

  • Visit the site and explore the various sections and links on the homepage.
  • You can find information about the vision, mission, and objectives of the General Education Department, as well as the various schemes and initiatives undertaken by the department for the school education in Kerala.
  • You can also access the government orders and circulars issued by the department on various matters related to the school education by clicking on the “GOs & Circulars” link.
  • You can also apply and manage various examinations and scholarships online by clicking on the “Online Services” link .
  • You can also contact the department and give your feedback by clicking on the “Contact Us” link .

Directorate of Higher Secondary Education

Description

  • You can find the vision, mission, and objectives of the DHSE, as well as the various schemes and initiatives undertaken by the DHSE for the higher secondary education in Kerala.
  • You can also access the government orders and circulars issued by the DHSE on various matters related to the higher secondary education.
  • You can also apply and manage various examinations and scholarships online, such as DHSE, VHSE, NSQF, Equivalency, etc.
  • You can also check the examination results and statistics of various courses and levels offered by the DHSE.

How to use them

  • Visit the site and select the examination that you want to check the results for.
  • Enter your roll number and date of birth and click on “Submit”.
  • You will see your result on the screen with your name, school, marks, grade, etc.
  • You can also download or print your result for future reference.
  • You can also view the schoolwise results, the list of selected candidates, and the instructions, notifications, and FAQs related to the examinations by clicking on the respective links on the homepage.
