Telangana School Education

Description

  • This website is the official portal of the Department of School Education in Telangana, India.
  • It provides various services and information related to school education in the state, such as:
  • Integrated School Management System (ISMS), which is a web-based application for managing and monitoring various aspects of school education, such as teacher recruitment, transfers, attendance, examinations, results, scholarships, etc12.
  • Student Information System (SIS), which is a web-based application for capturing and maintaining student data, such as admission, enrollment, attendance, performance, health, etc34.
  • Badi Bata Enrollment Entry, which is a web-based application for recording the enrollment of students in government schools for the academic year 2023-242.
  • Other useful links and resources, such as curriculum, textbooks, digital content, e-learning, etc.

How to Use:

  • Open the website in your browser and select the language of your choice from the top right corner.
  • On the homepage, you will see various tabs and icons for different services and information.
  • For example, if you want to use the Integrated School Management System (ISMS), you can click on the ISMS icon or the ISMS tab on the top menu bar.
  • Similarly, if you want to use the Student Information System (SIS), you can click on the SIS icon or the SIS tab on the top menu bar.
  • If you want to enter the enrollment data of students in government schools, you can click on the Badi Bata Enrollment Entry icon on the homepage.
  • If you need any help or guidance, you can click on the Help Desk icon on the homepage, or the Contact Us tab on the top menu bar.
  • You can also explore other useful links and resources on the website, such as curriculum, textbooks, digital content, e-learning, etc.

Description

  • which is the authority that regulates and supervises the system of intermediate education in the state of Telangana, India.
  • The website provides various services and information related to intermediate education in the state, such as:
  • Student online services, which include applying for various certificates, scholarships, grievances, etc1.
  • Government junior colleges online admissions, which allow students to apply for admission in government junior colleges in the state1.
  • Academic services, which include syllabus, textbooks, model question papers, practical question banks, academic calendar, etc1.

  • Notifications and announcements related to intermediate education13.

Telangana higher Education

Description

  • This website is the official portal of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE), which is a statutory body of the Government of Telangana State, India
  • The website lists the private universities that are established and recognized in the state, along with their details and notifications1.
  • The website displays the latest notifications and announcements related to higher education, such as entrance tests, admissions, scholarships, etc1.
  • The website provides the links to the official websites of various entrance tests that are conducted by TSCHE for admission into various courses, such as TS EAMCET2, TS ICET3, TS ECET4, etc.
  • The website also provides the information about the test zones, syllabus, user guides, mock tests, results, etc1.
  • The website provides the contact details of the department officials and the helpline numbers for any queries or grievances related to higher education1.

How to use them

  • Open the website and choose your language.
  • Click on the icons or tabs for the service or information you want.
  • Follow the instructions on the page to complete the process.
  • Contact the council or check the notifications for any help or updates.
