E-Pass Portal

Description

  • The website telanganaepass.cgg.gov.in is a scholarship portal for the state of Telangana, India.
  • Scholarships: The website offers various scholarships for SC, ST, BC, and EBC students.
  • Eligibility: It provides information about who is eligible, the courses that are eligible, and the colleges that are eligible.
  • Kalyana Lakshmi Scheme: This scheme is for marriage incentives and awards.
  • Skill Upgradation: The website provides services for skill upgradation like GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS.
  • Overseas Scholarship Services: It offers services for overseas scholarships.

How to Use:

  • Visit the Website: Go to the ePass website.
  • Select Scholarship Services: Click on scholarship services and select either post-matric or pre-matric1.
  • Enter Details: Enter the application number, academic year, SSC pass year, and SSC pass type. Also, enter your date of birth, and hall ticket number1.
  • Check Status: Click on the ‘Get Status’ button to check the status of your application1.
  • Apply for Scholarship: If you want to apply for a scholarship, you can fill out Application Status Forms online at the ePass official portal2.
  • Overseas Scholarship & Skill Upgradation: If you’re eligible for the Overseas scholarship & Skill Upgradation, you can apply through online registration process for fresh and renewal on the official website3.

TSCMFC:

Description

  • The website tscmfc.in/scholarships.php is the scholarship portal for the Telangana State Christian Minorities Finance Corporation. Here are some key points about the website:
  • State Government Scholarships: The website offers pre-matric scholarships for 1st to 10th grade and post-matric scholarships for Inter to PG. Full fee will be reimbursed and a scholarship of Rs.2,500/- is given1.
  • Central Government Scholarships: The website provides information about the Central Government Scholarships. Pre-matric scholarships of Rs.1,000/- for 1st to 5th & Rs.5,000/- for 6th to 10th. Post-matric scholarships for Inter to PG with a scholarship of Rs.2,500/- is given1.
  • Contact Information: The website provides contact information for the Commissioner/Director of Minorities Welfare.
  • CM’s Overseas Scholarship Scheme: The Government of Telangana provides financial assistance of an amount of Rs.20.00 Lakhs (Rupees Twenty Lakhs only) + One Way ticket fare to 50 eligible Christian minority students/graduates per year to pursue Post Graduate/Doctoral studies abroad2.
  • Success Stories: The website shares success stories of the beneficiaries of the CM’s Overseas Scholarship Scheme.
  • Application Procedure: The website provides a step-by-step guide on how to apply for the Overseas Scholarship.

How to use them:

  • Visit the Website: Go to the TSCMFC website1.
  • Select Scholarship Services: Click on the ‘Scholarships’ tab and select either ‘State Government Scholarships’ or 'Central Government Scholarships.
  • Enter Details: Enter the required details such as your academic year, SSC pass year, and SSC pass type. Also, enter your date of birth, and hall ticket number.
  • Check Status: Click on the ‘Get Status’ button to check the status of your application.
  • Apply for Scholarship: If you want to apply for a scholarship, you can fill out Application Status Forms online at the TSCMFC official portal.
  • CM’s Overseas Scholarship Scheme: If you’re eligible for the CM’s Overseas Scholarship Scheme, you can apply through online registration process for fresh and renewal on the official website.
