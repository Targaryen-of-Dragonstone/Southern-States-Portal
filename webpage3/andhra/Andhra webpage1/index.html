Andhra Pradesh Board of Education

Description

  • This website is the official site of the Andhra Pradesh Board of Education (APBE).
  • This is a board that executes and operates all major functions regarding the examination conduction for secondary and intermediate institutions in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
  • It also provides affiliation, accreditation, recognition and certification to schools, teachers, and students.
  • You can find information about the functions, organization chart, affiliation, admissions, results, and contact details of the APBE.
  • You can check the results of the AP HSC SSC Board exams for 10th and 12th classes by entering your roll number and date of birth.
  • You can access various student services such as scholarships, syllabus, model question papers, vocational courses, and photo gallery.
  • You can also learn about the Right to Information Act, the obligations of the public authority, the role of the central and state governments, and the procedure to request information.

How to Use:

  • 1. *Navigation:* The website typically had a straightforward layout. You could navigate through the site using the menu options or categories provided on the homepage. Look for sections or tabs dedicated to various subjects, classes, or specific resources.
  • 2. *Resources for Subjects and Classes:* Explore the sections dedicated to specific subjects or classes. You might find materials categorized by subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Languages (Tamil, English), etc., or by class levels such as 10th standard, 12th standard, and so on.
  • 3. *Study Materials:* Look for study materials, which could include textbooks, study guides, notes, worksheets, and sometimes video tutorials or presentations. These materials are generally aligned with the curriculum of the Tamil Nadu State Board.
  • 4. *Question Papers and Exam Resources:* Padasalai.net might offer access to previous years' question papers, sample papers, and exam tips to help students prepare for their exams.
  • 5. *Educational News and Updates:* The website may also provide educational news, announcements, or updates related to the educational system in Tamil Nadu.
  • 6. *Search Functionality:* If available, use the search bar to look for specific topics, subjects, or resources that you're interested in.

Comissioner of the School Education:

Description

  • The website you have entered is the official site of the Commissioner of School Education (CSE) of the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
  • The website provides information about the CSE and its functions, organization, and contact details.
  • The website offers online services for students, teachers, and schools such as information systems, textbooks, syllabus, and academic calendar.
  • The website updates the latest news, notifications, circulars, and orders related to the CSE and its schemes and programs.
  • The website also links to the sub-sites of the CSE such as Directorate of Government Exams, Mana Badi Nadu Nedu, and Right to Information.

How to use them:

  • Click on About Us to learn about the CSE and its functions, organization, and contact details.
  • Click on Online Services to access online services for students, teachers, and schools such as information systems, textbooks, syllabus, and academic calendar.
  • Click on Updates to view the latest news, notifications, circulars, and orders related to the CSE and its schemes and programs.
  • Click on Sub Sites to visit the sub-sites of the CSE such as Directorate of Government Exams, Mana Badi Nadu Nedu, and Right to Information.

Intermediate Education

Description
