Jnana Bhumi

Description

  • This website is the official site of JnanaBhumi, a smart portal for integrated delivery of services relating to education and scholarships in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
  • This is a platform that provides financial assistance to students belonging to various social and economic backgrounds for pursuing higher education.
  • structure, and contact details of JnanaBhumi.
  • The website offers online services for students, colleges, and teachers such as student information system, school information system, teacher information system, and school management information system.
  • The website allows students to apply online for various scholarships such as Jagananna Vidya Deevena, Jagananna Vasathi Deevena, and Jagananna Civil Services Prothsahakam.
  • The website updates the latest alerts, circulars, notifications, and orders related to JnanaBhumi and its schemes and programs.

How to Use:

  • Click on About Us to learn about the vision, mission, objectives, functions, organization structure, and contact details of JnanaBhumi.
  • Click on Online Services to access various online services for students, colleges, and teachers such as student information system, school information system, teacher information system, and school management information system.
  • Click on Scholarships to apply online for various scholarships such as Jagananna Vidya Deevena, Jagananna Vasathi Deevena, and Jagananna Civil Services Prothsahakam.
  • Click on Updates to view the latest alerts, circulars, notifications, and orders related to JnanaBhumi and its schemes and programs.
  • Click on Sub Sites to visit the sub-sites of JnanaBhumi such as Directorate of Government Exams, Mana Badi Nadu Nedu, and Right to Information.
