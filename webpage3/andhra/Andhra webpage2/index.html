Andhra State Bank

Description

  • This website is the official site of the Andhra Pradesh State Co-operative Bank Ltd. (APCOB).
  • This is a bank that provides financial services to the co-operative sector in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
  • It also offers various products and services to its customers, such as accounts, deposits, loans, digital banking, and others.
  • The website also links to the sub-sites of the APCOB such as Directorate of Government Exams, Mana Badi Nadu Nedu, and Right to Information.

How to Use:

  • Click on About Us to learn about the APCOB and its functions, organization, and contact details.
  • Click on Online Services to access various online services such as net banking, mobile banking, e-passbook, and e-statement.
  • Click on Products to view the various products and services offered by the APCOB such as accounts, deposits, loans, insurance, and others.
  • Click on Recruitment to apply online for the post of Staff Assistant in the APCOB for the fiscal year 2023-24. The last date to apply is 31 October 2023.
  • Click on Updates to view the latest updates, alerts, circulars, notifications, and orders related to the APCOB and its schemes and programs.
  • Click on Sub Sites to visit the sub-sites of the APCOB such as Directorate of Government Exams, Mana Badi Nadu Nedu, and Right to Information.
